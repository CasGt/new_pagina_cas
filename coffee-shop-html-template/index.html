About Us

Serving Since 1995

With the purpose of helping find solutions to the country's problems, CAS and Campus Sur were created to strengthen the educational field. CAS was authorized by the Ministry of Education in January 1995, providing flexibility in its procedures and programs, which allows for the development of curriculum and evaluation as well as teacher training. CAS has been providing academic excellence for over 25 years to enhance education in Guatemala.

Our Mision

To educate students, guiding the development of their bilingual, academic, social and professional competencies and fostering growth in their values as citizens committed to society and the environment.kills, committed to the development of their communities.

Learn More

Our Vision

To be the leader of innovative education in the South Coast region of Guatemala, providing students with a quality academic experience that prepares them for higher education and global citizenship, committed to the development of their communities.

Learn More

Definition of learning

Learning in CAS is a transformative process in which knowledge is acquired, consciousness is awakened, and skills are obtained that empower students to improve and fully engage with their communities, their country, and the world through critical thinking, problem-solving, and collaboration.

Our initiatives

With the aim of preparing bilingual leaders with critical thinking skills and a commitment to the development of their community, we offer the best quality experience that prepares them for higher education and global citizenship.

CHESS

CAS promotes chess within clubs for children from 4th grade and onwards, and it is also offered as an extracurricular activity for parents and teachers.

ROBOTICS

The ideal proposal for learning through play and promoting creativity, developing mathematical logic, spatial organization, directionality, and creative thinking.

NEWSELA

A powerful learning tool with personalized instruction that provides exclusive reading resources.

KHAN ACADEMY

A virtual resource aimed at developing or enriching learning in subjects such as mathematics, chemistry, and programming. The student learns at their own pace, and it provides valuable information to the teacher about the progress of their students.

MORNING MEETING

It is a 20-minute block in the morning where students can gather with their guide teacher to discuss upcoming events, CAS values, and also engage in silent or group reading. It is a time to organize and prepare for a day of learning.
